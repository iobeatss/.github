## 🗓 Global Roadmap
> **All roadmap milestones are proposed & approved via DAO (Snapshot).**

### ✅ Q3 2025 — Foundation & Transparency
- Multi-chain presale (Base, BNB, ETH, Polygon, Arbitrum)  
- Safe Multisig (4/7) + Sablier vesting  
- Liquidity activation per chain (Gamma / Beefy)  
- Transparency docs (Appendix B, locks, proofs)  
- Whitepaper (Tokenomics & Governance) draft

### 🚀 Q4 2025 — DAO & Mobile
- DAO Governance launch (Snapshot)  
- NFT Creator Studio + Artist Verification  
- Android App (Beta) + iOS submission  
- Rooftop Tour Canada announcement & sponsors

### 🟡 Q1 2026 — DeFi & Marketplace
- Vaults & Staking (Beefy + Gamma)  
- Cross-chain NFT Marketplace  
- Dynamic NFT royalties  
- Loyalty program for holders  
- Tier-2 CEX listings (MEXC, Gate, Bitmart)

### 🌍 Q2 2026 — Scale & Events
- Rooftop Tour Canada (MTL, TOR, VAN)  
- Live NFT drops at events  
- Tier-1 CEX listings (Binance, Coinbase, Bybit, OKX, KuCoin)  
- Festival & brand partnerships  
- AI recommendations + NFT fan clubs
